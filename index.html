From Farm to Flame

The journey of a true Cowboy Smoke.

  • ✓ Seed: Criollo '98 seed planted in rich volcanic loam.
  • ✓ Seedling: Transplanted to Lot 12A under shade.
  • ✓ Harvest: Leaves primed by hand at peak ripeness.
  • ✓ Cure: Air-cured in Secadero 3 for 45 days.
  • ✓ Ferment: Slow pilon fermentation, turned weekly.
  • ✓ Aged: Rested 18 months in Spanish cedar.
  • ✓ Finish: Draw tested, banded, and packed by hand.
